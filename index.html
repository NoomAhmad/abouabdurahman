About US

Abu Abdulrahman Foundation Charity was established more than ten years ago and its founder is Abu Abdulrahman, may Allah Almighty honor him. Thanks to Allah, many charity projects have been accomplished by this charity foundation.. such as digging wells, building mosques, feeding the poor, sponsoring orphans, helping widows and divorcees, providing care for the elderly and people with disabilities, providing treatment for the sick, and providing food and clothing also education.. You can suppory to help thousands of needy people around the world by donating to them. Just click on the donate button and your donation will reach them directly
